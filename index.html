FREQUENCY GATE   x   HOT - COLD

×

sound: pitch change
frequency gate is an audio filter allowing to 'cut-off' certain frequencies (unit: Hz) from an audio sample. Setting a 'gate' Hz value, only frequencies above (high-pass) or below it (low-pass) can 'pass' through and be heard. The filtered audio sounds dimmer or thinner (cut), in comparison to the richer original sample.

refers to inducing a change in temperature or climate.
